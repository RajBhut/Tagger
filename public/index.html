How to Play:

  • Use arrow keys or on-screen controls to move your player
  • If you are the tagger (red), chase and tag other players
  • If you don't tag someone within 10 seconds, you're eliminated!
  • Earn Points:
    • Quick tags earn up to 5 points
    • Survive until the end for a +10 point bonus
  • Collect power-ups for special abilities:
    • ● Speed - Move faster
    • ● Shield - Can't be tagged
    • ● Freeze - Freeze other players
  • Last player standing or highest score wins!
